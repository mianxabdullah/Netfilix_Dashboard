Interactive Portfolio Project

Netflix Catalog Architecture & Analytics

An advanced interactive 3-page Business Intelligence ecosystem tracking over 8,800 titles to diagnose content volume patterns, macro distributions, and global talent partnerships.

Analytical Scope & Architecture

This deployment translates structural catalog patterns into business insights. The reporting environment is built natively across three balanced viewpoints to allow corporate decision-makers to interact seamlessly with dynamic filters.

  • Macro Volume Overview: Tracks global production footprints alongside historic release trajectories over cross-filtered timelines.
  • Content Diagnostics: Segregates maturity ratings (dominated by TV-MA and TV-14 boundaries) against movie runtime classifications.
  • Creative Talent Clusters: Isolates heavy-hitting industry actors and structural director duos using granular context filters.
